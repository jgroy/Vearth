# Vearth 🌍

## What is Vearth?
Vearth is a virtual world that mirrors real life — 
where social life, shopping, gaming, and human 
connection all exist in one seamless place, 
on a global scale.

## The Problem
Today we have an app for social life, an app for 
shopping, an app for food, an app for gaming, an app 
for finding people to play with or make connection — many apps, no real 
correlation. Vearth solves this.

## Core Features
- Social connectivity and communities
- Shopping and local discovery  
- Gaming and interest based matchmaking
- Event planning and hangouts
- Seamless all-in-one digital presence

## Vision
To cover all digital presence under a single platform 
and make digital life as seamless and natural as 
real life — with each day feeling real.


## Current Status
Early development - building the foundation.
